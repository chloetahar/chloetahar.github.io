About me



    My background is in literary studies as well as social sciences. I have previously enrolled in a three-years "preparatory courses" tracks in humanities, with a specialization in French Language and Literature, pursued with a M.A. in French Language at the ENS Lyon (M1) and at the EHESS in Paris (M2). I retain a strong interest in understanding how language contributes to the shaping of our socio-historical prototypes and stereotypes.
