About me

    After my bachelor, I have spent two years studying Graphic arts at Ecole Estienne. Then, I enrolled in a three-years programme called "preparatory classes", where I specialized in Modern French Literature, to pursue with a Master of Arts in French Language at the ENS Lyon and at the EHESS in Paris. From my social sciences and literary background, I keep a strong interest in understanding how language shapes our social and cultural prototypes.
