Hello! my name is Chloe Tahar.

I'm a PhD student in linguistics.


I’m currently finishing my dissertation: "Expletive Negation: from imperatives to connectives. A formal diachronic approach/ La négation explétive: des impératifs aux connecteurs. Une approche de diachronie formelle" at the Institut Jean Nicod of the Ecole Normale Supérieure. I'm advised by Alda Mari.

My main research interests are in formal semantics and pragmatics, and in the ways these research methods can be applied to the description of historical data.

I'm also interested in building bridges between usage-based approaches and strictly logical approaches to natural language meaning. To that end, I use corpus-based methods of investigation as the foundation for further theoretical analysis.
