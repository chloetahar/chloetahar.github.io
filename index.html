Hello! my name is Chloe Tahar.

I'm a PhD student in linguistics.

Thanks for reading. I’m currently finishing my dissertation: "Expletive Negation: from imperatives to connectives. A formal diachronic approach", under the supervision of Alda Mari at the Institut Jean Nicod (Ecole Normale Supérieure). You can check my CV here.

My main research interests are in formal semantics and its interface with pragmatics, with a specific interest for the semantics of clause-types, attitude verbs, as well as connectives and for diagnosing projective content.

Empirically, I’m exploring (diachronic) corpus investigation methods, as well as research methods from historical and comparative linguistics (with a focus on Indo-European languages).
