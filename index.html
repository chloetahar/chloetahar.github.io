Hello! my name is Chloe Tahar.

I'm a PhD student in linguistics.

Thanks for reading. I’m currently finishing my dissertation: "Expletive Negation: from imperatives to connectives. A formal diachronic approach/ La négation explétive: des impératifs aux connecteurs. Une approche de diachronie formelle" under the supervision of Alda Mari at the Institut Jean Nicod of the Ecole Normale Supérieure. You can check my CV here.

My work applies the methods of formal semantics and pragmatics to the description of historical data. I am interested in building bridges between usage-based approaches and strictly logical approaches to natural language meaning, with a focus on lexical meaning (e.g., that of attitude verbs) and grammatical meaning (e.g., that of connectives).

Empirically, I use corpus-based methods of investigation as the foundation for further semantic tests and theoretical analysis.
