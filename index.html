I'm a postdoctoral researcher in linguistics at the Institut des sciences du langage of the Université de Neuchâtel. Before that, I was working as a research engineer at the Université d'Orléans on the oral corpus of spoken interaction ESLO. I defended my dissertation: "Expletive Negation: from imperatives to connectives. A formal diachronic approach", written under the supervision of Alda Mari, in April 2022, at the Institut Jean Nicod of the Ecole Normale Supérieure.

My research focuses on semantics and the semantics-pragmatics interface, using methods from corpus linguistics. I study more specifically expletive negation, both in a broad approach to this phenomenon and by focusing on the history of French. Additionally, I employ the methods of textual data analysis with the aim of testing, revising and refining existing hypotheses on attitude reports.
